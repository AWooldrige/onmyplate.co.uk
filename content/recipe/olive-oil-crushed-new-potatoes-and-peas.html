--- title: Olive Oil Crushed New Potatoes and Peas kind: recipe created_at: 2013-01-07 18:07:48 +0000 Summary: New potatoes can be dull. Throw in a few cheap ingredients and liven them up with these fresh, summery additions. Cook Time: PT15M - 15 minutes Prep Time: PT10M - 10 minutes Yield: 4 portions as a side dish Cuisine: Mixed Category: Side Dish Legacy WP ID: 201 Ingredients: Main: - New Potatoes, 800 g - Olive Oil, 200 ml - Frozen Garden Peas, 2 cups - Sea salt, 1/2 tsp - Lemon Juice, 1/2 lemon - Ground Black Pepper, 1 tsp - Ground White Pepper, 1/4 tsp Method: - Boil the new potatoes in a pan until tender (about 20 minutes). Add the peas for the last 5 minutes of cooking. - Drain and add the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, white pepper and black pepper to the pan. - Crush gently with a fork and serve immediately. ---
